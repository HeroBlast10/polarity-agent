# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective date:** 2026-03-05
**Last updated:** 2026-03-05

---

## 1. Nature of the Project

**Polarity Agent** is an open-source software framework released under the
[MIT License](LICENSE). It is designed as a **satirical, entertainment-oriented
tool for exploring the boundaries of logical reasoning, rhetorical fallacies,
and emotional polarity in AI-generated dialogue**.

The personas shipped with this framework — including but not limited to
*Advocatus* (the unconditional supporter) and *Inquisitor / Troll* (the
unconditional contrarian) — are **intentionally exaggerated caricatures**
created for comedic, educational, and stress-testing purposes. They do **not**
represent the views, beliefs, or endorsements of the developers.

## 2. Intended Use

This software is intended **solely** for:

- Entertainment and humor.
- Academic or educational exploration of logical fallacies and rhetorical
  strategies.
- Stress-testing and red-teaming of LLM alignment boundaries.
- Creative writing, improv comedy, and meme generation.

## 3. Prohibited Use

You **shall not** use Polarity Agent to:

1. Generate content that incites **violence, terrorism, or hatred** against any
   individual or group.
2. Produce **child sexual abuse material (CSAM)** or any content that exploits
   minors.
3. Create **targeted harassment, doxxing, or defamation** of real persons.
4. Distribute **disinformation** with the intent to deceive or manipulate
   public opinion in a way that causes tangible harm.
5. Violate any **applicable local, national, or international law**.
